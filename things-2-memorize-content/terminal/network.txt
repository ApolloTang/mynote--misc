

Show process tying up a port 3000 (OSx)
````````````````````````````````````````
sudo lsof -i tcp:3000


Show list of used port (OSx)
````````````````````````````
sudo netstat -atp tcp | grep -i "listen"





2 ways to show wired/ethernet address
-------------------------------------
$ ipconfig getifaddr en0
192.168.0.89

$ ifconfig | grep inet
