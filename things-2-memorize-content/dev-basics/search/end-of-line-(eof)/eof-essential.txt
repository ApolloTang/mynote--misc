
line ending of different OS
----------------------------

  CRLF  \r\n  Windows
  LF    \n    Linux
  LF    \n    Macintosh (Mac OSX)
  CR    \r    Macintosh (old)

  Ref:
  https://confluence.qps.nl/fledermaus7/questions-answers/other/differences-in-end-of-line-characters-mac-windows-and-linux



Representation of EOL
---------------------

                     | char | c  |  Hex | uni-code | Oct | Dec |
    Charriage return | [CR] | \r | \x0D | U+000D   |  15 |  13 |
  Line feed/New Line | [LF] | \n | \x0A | U+000A   |  12 |  10 |
