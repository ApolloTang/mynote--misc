
Find name contain 'cookie' but not in node_module folder

  find . \( -name *cookie* \) -not \( -path */node_modules/* \)

The following is fast:

  find .  -not \( -path *node_modules* -prune \) -not \( -path *styling* -prune \) -and \( -name *cookie* \)
