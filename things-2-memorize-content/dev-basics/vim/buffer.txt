
:bufdo! bd   - deletes all buffers except those with unwritten changes.
:bufdo! bw   - like bd, but "really" delete the buffer (help :bw).


:sba        - open all buffer in horizontal split [1]
:vert sba   - open all buffer in vertical split [1]

:new      - new empthy buffer in horizontal split [2]
:vnew     - new empthy buffer in vertical split [2]
