[Cmd-.]        Quick Fixes: show available options to fix thing.

[Cmd-Sft-Spc]  Trigger function's actual-arguments hints. Ref:[1][2]
               Place cursor in the fuction parenthesis and press [Cmd-Sft-Spc] (window:[Ctr-Sft-Spc]).
               This is same as [Cmd-Sft-p]Trigg.
               This is called "@command:editor.action.triggerParameterHints" in Keyboard Shortcuts.
