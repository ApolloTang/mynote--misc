Chrome binary locations on mac:

  /Applications/Google\ Chrome.app/Contents/MacOS


Chrome store profile locations on Mac:
  ~/Library/Application\ Support/Google/Chrome


How to launch chrome with specific profile from command line:
  /Applications/Google\ Chrome.app/Contents/MacOS/Google\ Chrome --new-window --args --profile-directory="Profile 1" $* 2>&1 &
