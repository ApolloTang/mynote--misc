tpop/vim-commentary

  gcc: comment out a line (takes a count)

  gc :
    (normal)
      comment out the target of a motion (for example, gcap to comment out a paragraph)
    (visual mode)
      to comment out the selection
    (operator pending mode)
      gc : to target a comment.

  use it as a command:
    :7,17Commentary
    :g/TODO/Commentary.
