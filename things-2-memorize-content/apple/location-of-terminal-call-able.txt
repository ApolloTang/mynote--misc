

In OSX many program names that you'll find in the follwoing folder
can be called via a CLI from the shell.

/Applications
/Applications/Utilities,
/System/Library/CoreServices/Applications (as well as other things under the
/System

Some custom programs under the classic Unix locations is also callable:

/usr/bin
/usr/local/bin
/opt
/sys/bin
