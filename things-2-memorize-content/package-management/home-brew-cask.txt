================
Listing packages
================

  brew cask list


==============
Remove package
==============

  brew cask uninstall <pkg-name>


===============
upgrade package
===============

  brew cask reinstall  <pkg-name>

  or

  use: https://github.com/buo/homebrew-cask-upgrade
  [ref] https://apple.stackexchange.com/a/253717/282542
